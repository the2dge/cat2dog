Developed with expert cat nutritionists, with a focus on the most common cat health problems such as urinary tract issues and hairballs.

THE SCIENCE

Cats are susceptible to the formation of different types of stones (uroliths) in the urinary tract, particularly if the urine pH is too high (> 6.6) or too low (< 6.0). Our recipes have been formulated with added methionine, which together with the help of positively and negatively charged minerals supports your cat’s urinary tract health by maintaining urine pH within an ideal range. The use of beet pulp and cellulose helps to support the reduction of hairballs. The combination of both these ingredients helps to gently move hair through the gastrointestinal tract while maintaining healthy digestion. Beet pulp, a moderately fermentable fibre, promotes optimal intestinal health. Cellulose helps to enhance the passage of ingested hair. These fibre sources help bind the hairs that cats ingest while grooming and help them pass down the digestive tract and out in faeces rather than vomiting up hairballs.

貓咪容易在尿道中形成不同類型的結石（尿石），特別是當尿液的pH值過高 （> 6.6）或過低（< 6.0）時。我們的配方添加了美硫氨酸，這與正負電荷的礦物質共同作用，有助於維持貓咪尿液pH值在理想範圍內，從而支持尿道健康。 此外，使用甜菜漿和纖維素有助於減少毛球的形成。這兩種成分的結合可以輕柔地將毛發推動通過腸胃道，同時保持健康的消化。甜菜漿是一種中等發酵的纖維，有助於促進最佳的腸道健康。纖維素則有助於加速攝取的毛發的通過。 這些纖維來源有助於綁住貓咪在梳理時吞嚥的毛發， 並幫助其通過消化道， 最終以糞便排出，而不是嘔吐出毛球。
